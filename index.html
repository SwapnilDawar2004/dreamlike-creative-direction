04 — north stars

five people whose work i study and pull from.

01

apple design films

product launches

what i take: saying less to communicate more.

what i'd use: if it needs explaining, the visuals failed.

02

christopher nolan

inception, interstellar, the dark knight

what i take: using pacing to control attention.

what i'd use: let the cuts carry the emotion, not the music.

03

denis villeneuve

dune, blade runner 2049, arrival

what i take: making environment do the storytelling.

what i'd use: negative space is content, not filler.

04

joaquim dos santos

spider-man: across the spider-verse

what i take: using visual inconsistency as a storytelling tool.

what i'd use: style shifts as information, not decoration.

05

robert valley

"zima blue"

what i take: knowing what to remove.

what i'd use: if removing it doesn't hurt, it shouldn't be there.

experiencing and evolving
